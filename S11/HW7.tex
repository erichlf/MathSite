	$f(3)=6,\, f'(3)=0,\, f(1)=1,\, f'(1)=5,\, g(3)=1,\, g'(3)=2,\, g(4)=2,\,
	g'(4)=1,\, h(3)=4, \text{ and } h'(3)=2$
